About our test

The Braunaghatta Personality Test (BPT) was constructed over the course of over 15 years of research, and was further developed as part of an international collaborative effort by the world's most impactful cognitive and psycho-social science laboratories. Since its publication, this test has quickly become widely accepted as the field standard. While it shares a bit of foundational theory with the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) and the Newcastle Personality Assessor (NPA), the BPT is fundamentally revolutionary in that it utilizes modern day advancements in machine learning and artificial neural networks in order to construct a powerfully accurate and in-depth analysis of the test subject's psyche. As of today, over 200 scholarly articles have been written on the theory behind the BPT. We hope you enjoy taking our assessment and learning more about yourself.
